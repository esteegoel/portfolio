11 Years

Rowing

I'm a National champion & internationally competive rower over 10 years and 5 different clubs (New York Athletic Club, Carnegie Mellon, Montclair HS, ReadySetRow, and Neried BC)

As the "ultimate team sport," my favorite part of rowing is the camraderie it brings and the wonderful people I have met!

Rowing has certainly shaped who I am, and many of the key lesson have been carried into the designs I create.


7 Years

Small Business

In 2018, I started making custom items for friends and family. Now, I create home murals, design t-shirts, make cards, and sell custom drawings as stickers and prints.

Then in April 2020, during the Covid-19 Pandemic, I created an Instagram page to sell my products, but have since expanded as it received much more attention than I could ever imagine!




18 Years

Artwork

At three, I seemed to be good at it. The pre-school teacher used my work to exemplify “creativity and craftsmanship.” In elementary school, there was wider recognition -- work displayed in hallways around the school.

In middle school, there were awards from shows and competitions. I made a portfolio to place into the honors art track for high school, designing logos for various school clubs, decorations and posters for events. I was given my first gallery and received a personal request from our Congresswoman.

In college, I designed multi-level booths that won university-wide prizes, and continued to pursue what I love.
