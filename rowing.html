My rowing journey began in July of 2014 when I was signed up up for a "learn to row" camp. as a soccer player, I was completely uninterested. Used to high-energy competitive sports, sitting in a boat and rowing down a river sounded completely boring. but after day one, I was hooked, and soon dropped soccer to spend the next 9 years obsessed with rowing.

Rowing has certainly shaped who I am, and many of the key lesson have been carried into the designs I create.

Most recently, I served as president & women's captain, of the CMU Rowing team.
